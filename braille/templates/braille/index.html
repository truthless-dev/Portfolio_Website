To make your unicode Braille, simply navigate to the edit field below and start typing!

You can type dots 1, 2, and 3 with the 'f', 'd', and 's' keys, respectively. 'j', 'k', and 'l' are your dots 4, 5, and 6. Finally, dots 7 and 8 are typed with 'a' and ';' keys, in the event you need to enter computer Braille. Use the backspace and enter keys to delete your last cell and insert a newline, respectively.

You can use common keystrokes like CTRL+A and CTRL+C within the field as usual, but any print text will be rejected to avoid mingling print and Braille.
